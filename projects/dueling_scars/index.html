Dueling Scars

a single page swordplay dice game for two players with 30 minutes, released April 2022

Dueling Scars is a fast, simple, single-page swordplay dice game for two players with 30 minutes. Evoking actual fencing tactical concepts, this game uses a d6 dice pool resolution system and a simplified version of position and effect from Forged in the Dark to drive daring strategic combat between two players.

This game is best played when printed on an A4 or 8.3 x 11.7 inches (210 x 297 mm)​ sheet of paper, with 8 ~ 12 physical d6 dice and two tokens. It was developed in April 2022 as a mechanical proof-of-concept, and I would love to hear about your experience playing it in the comments below.
